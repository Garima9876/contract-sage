MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

This Memorandum of Understanding (hereinafter referred to as the "MOU") is entered into on this 5th day of April, 2025, by and between:

PARTY A: XYZ Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 123, Nehru Place, New Delhi - 110019 (hereinafter referred to as “First Party”);

AND

PARTY B: ABC & Co., a partnership firm registered under the Indian Partnership Act, 1932, having its principal place of business at 456, MG Road, Bengaluru - 560001 (hereinafter referred to as “Second Party”).

WHEREAS both parties are desirous of collaborating for the purpose of providing joint legal consultancy and compliance solutions in India;

NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. Scope of Collaboration:
   The First Party and the Second Party agree to jointly develop and deliver legal and regulatory consultancy services, share resources and expertise, and work in coordination for mutual benefit.

2. Confidentiality:
   Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all shared data, documents, and information exchanged in the course of this engagement.

3. Validity:
   This MOU shall remain valid for a period of 12 (twelve) months from the date of execution unless terminated earlier by mutual consent.

4. Dispute Resolution:
   Any disputes arising out of this MOU shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in New Delhi, India.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have executed this MOU on the day and year first above written.
